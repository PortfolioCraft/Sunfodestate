Frequently Asked Questions.

How do I know the land is genuine before buying?

We conduct thorough land searches to verify ownership and ensure the property is free from legal issues.

How do I book a house viewing?

Simply contact us, and we will schedule a viewing at your convenience.

What documents are required for a title transfer?

You need a sale agreement, original title deed, KRA PIN, and ID copies of both the buyer and seller.

How early should I book for an event?

We recommend booking at least a week in advance to secure availability.

How long does the title transfer process take?

On average, it takes 60-90 days, depending on government processing times.

What does your property management service include?
